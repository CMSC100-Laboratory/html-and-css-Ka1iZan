About Me


Bien Gabriel J. Mainit is a junior computer science student at the University of the Philippines Los Baños. Hailing from the city of Pasig, he completed his secondary education at Pasig City Science High School, graduating with high honors in junior, and honors in senior high school. With a strong interest in data science and cybersecurity, Bien is actively building his skills through coursework and side-projects. More than anything, Bien is passionate about leveraging technology to solve real-world problems, and is looking forward to grow within the field through varying opportunities. Aside from programming, Bien has been part of several organizations such as the Elite Debate Society club and currently, the UPLB Computer Science Society, serving as the latter’s MicroCOSSm’s Committee head.
